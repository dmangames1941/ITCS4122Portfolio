Project 1: Uber Ride Data Visualization

For this project I wanted to look at the distribution of the data in the Uber Ride dataset from Kaggle.

Section 1: Booking Status Time Based Visuals

This section looks at how many of each booking status took place throught a 24 hour period. The visual is broken down into 20 minute increments and allows us to see when the peak busiest time for Uber is throught the day.

Booking Status Column Distribution

Section 2: Variable Distribution

This section looks at the percent distribution of the main variables looked at in this project. We can see how many rides are completed, incompleted, and cancelled as well as the reasons rides were cancelled or not completed. Lastly we see a distribution of customer ratings. This allows us to see the efficiency of Uber and what can be focused on the provide the best customer and worker experience.

Section 3: Quarterly Customer Ratings

In this section we look at the average customer rating throughout the year. We also see the minimum, maximum, and average for each quarter allowing our workers to better service customers year long.
